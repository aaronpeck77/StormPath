StormPath Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 15, 2026

Overview

This policy explains what data StormPath uses while you navigate and how that data is handled. By using StormPath, you agree to this policy.

Data We Process

  • Location data: used to show your position, build routes, and compute route conditions.
  • App diagnostics: app version, feature toggles, and provider availability for support troubleshooting.
  • On-device route learning data: frequent-route signals and activity dots, stored on your device.

Third-Party Services

Depending on your configuration and region, StormPath may request data from:

  • Mapbox (maps, traffic overlays, routing features)
  • OpenRouteService (route generation)
  • OpenWeather (weather hints along route)
  • US National Weather Service, api.weather.gov (US weather alerts)

Requests to those services are subject to their own privacy terms and policies.

How Diagnostics Work

The Support Diagnostics button creates text locally and copies it to your clipboard. If you choose to email support, your default email app opens and you decide what to send.

Data Retention and Control

  • Frequent-route learning data is stored locally on your device.
  • You can clear trail/learning data from the About panel.
  • You can disable optional features (traffic, weather hints, voice, etc.) in settings.
